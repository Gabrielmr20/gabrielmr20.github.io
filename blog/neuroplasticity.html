How the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people

  • Gives people the ability to learn new things.
  • The ability to enhance existing cognitive capabilities.
  • Recovery from strokes and traumatic brain injuries.
  • Strengthening areas if some functions are lost or decline.
  • Improvements that can boost brain fitness.

Neuroplasticity principles for my own benefit

Technically speaking I'm constantly using all the benefits from the moment I was born until now; the brain does that without thinking about, is how we learn to walk, speak and all the rest.


Some ways to increase neuroplasticity

  • Learn a new language
  • Learn to play an instrument
  • Visit a different part of the city, state or country
  • Go to a museum or concert
  • Cook new foods or listen to different music
  • Practice mindfulness
  • Experiment with three good things
  • Try brain aerobics:
    • Use your alternate hand to brush your teeth or write
    • Dress while keeping the eyes closed
    • Buy groceries at a different store

What it is and why Growth Mindset it is relevant.

Growth mindset describes a way of viewing challenges and setbacks. People who have a growth mindset believe that even if they struggle with certain skills, their abilities aren't set in stone. They think that with work, their skills can improve over time.
