How culture influenced my Identity

I was born in Brazil and as a Brazilian, a key part of my personality is the willingness to laugh about ourselves, our predicaments and political absurdities and to welcome others in on the joke.

A few years back, Brazilians voted a clown into Congress. What was unusual was that he was a real clown by profession. Francisco Everardo Oliveira Silva, known by his stage name of "Grumpy" in Portuguese, won on a slogan, "It can't get any worse". So, from there you can estipulate that we don't set limits for jokes.

Having the mindset of "It can't get any worse". The only path ahead is improvement even if it means to make some really doubtful choices.

Hey, the clown actually did good!

What are my values?

Respect for others and for myself, to respect and be respected.

Honesty and sincerity not ignoring respect.

Integrity to keep by those values, it can be called “value” but that does not mean it have a price.

What are my strengths?

I know a little about a lot, a machinegun of random facts, able to link completely non relate subjects.

There is no way to explain the process, but I can “show” how it works with a game that I've called 7 clicks for the stars!

Is a simple game, if you have a smartphone or a computer with internet connection you can play it. the score is set like golf, less is more, get a random subject on Wikipedia like “The golden bird” and using just the links inside Wikipedia (you can't use external links or google and can't go back after going inside a link) try to reach “Seven Dwarfs”, count each link that you access (this is your score), so CTRL+F for words that maybe are a link, just a hint: dwarfs dig for GOLD…

I use this on every aspect of my communication, creating bridges between me and another person and keep rich conversations while I learn a little more with this interaction.

So, summarizing my strengths are “sponge brain” and human skills.
